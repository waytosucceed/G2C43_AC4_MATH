How to Arrange Numbers Vertically for Multiplication:

When multiplying numbers like 21 × 3, it’s important to align the digits properly in vertical format to make the calculation easy.

Follow these steps:

Step 1: Write the Numbers Vertically

  1. Write the larger number (21) on top.
  2. Write the smaller number (3) directly below it.
  3. Align the digits by their place values (ones under ones, tens under tens).
